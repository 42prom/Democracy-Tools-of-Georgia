# Privacy Policy for Democracy Tools of Georgia (DTG)

**Effective Date**: 2026-02-19

## 1. Overview

Democracy Tools of Georgia ("DTG", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This app is designed to facilitate secure, verifiable, and anonymous voting. We prioritize data minimization and user sovereignty.

## 2. Data Collection & Usage

### 2.1 Identity Verification (NFC & Biometrics)

- **Data Collected**: Document data from eMRTD (Passport/ID) via NFC, and facial biometrics (Selfie).
- **Purpose**: To verify your eligibility to vote and prevent impersonation.
- **Storage**: Biometric templates are processed securely. We do not store raw biometric images permanently after verification unless required for audit logs (encrypted).
- **Third Parties**: We do not share your identity data with third parties for marketing purposes.

### 2.2 Voting Data

- **Data Collected**: Your encrypted vote preference.
- **Privacy**: Your vote is cryptographically decoupled from your identity using Zero-Knowledge Proofs/Nullifiers.
- **Storage**: Votes are stored on a public/private blockchain ledger for integrity. It is mathematically impossible to link a vote back to your identity.

### 3. Device Permissions

This app requires certain permissions to function:

- **Camera**: Used for scanning QR codes and liveness verification.
- **NFC**: Used for reading government-issued ID documents.
- **Microphone**: May be used during video liveness checks (if applicable).
- **Photo Library**: Used if you choose to upload a profile picture.

## 4. Data Security

We employ military-grade encryption (AES-256) and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. Your private keys are stored in your device's secure enclave where supported.
